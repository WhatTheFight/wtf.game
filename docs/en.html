HOW TO PLAY

THREE PHASES. 10 WEEKS. ONE GOAL: DEFEND YOUR SIDE.

PHASE 1 — PICK YOUR SIDE
▶Join Side A (Trump Free) or Side B (Pro-Trump)
▶Buy the matching token on Pump.fun
▶Lock your tokens in your side's Chest (MVP: public multisig wallet)
PHASE 2 — WEEKLY DEBATES & VOTES
▶Every week: debate topics + voting rounds (Discord / community)
▶Your lock in the Chest = participation power (weight)
▶Every Sunday: judgment + penalty applied to the losing side
▶MVP: settlement executed via public multisig + on-chain transparency
PHASE 3 — SEASON WINNER
▶Winner determined by TWAP (50%) + Chest Weight (50%)
▶MVP Pump.fun: no on-chain burn — tokens keep on living
▶Post-MVP WTFight Protocol: automatic full burn of the losing token (future feature)
▶Debates have existed since the dawn of time: with a decentralized protocol, arenas and topics are infinite
TWAP Rule (7 days / 168 data points): price is sampled every hour over 7 days = 168 points (7×24). TWAP is the average of these 168 values (window: Monday 00:00 UTC → Sunday 23:59 UTC).
