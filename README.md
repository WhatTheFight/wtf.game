## The Rules of Engagement

It's simple: **Buy the camp you believe in.**

Tokens are minted live on a bonding curve. There is no lock-up, no waiting period. If your conviction dies, you can sell your position immediately.

But remember: **To hold a token is to raise your hand.** Your capital isn't just a bet; it's your voice in the arena. The more you hold, the louder you are.

The **Conviction Index** runs the numbers in the background ‚ÄĒ analyzing the TWAP trend, the net buy pressure from believers like you, and the volatility of the fight. It's a real-time pulse on which side is putting their money where their mouth is.

And because this is the Ledger, **nothing expires.** Every trade, every shift in conviction, is recorded forever. Tokens accumulate history, creating a permanent record of a belief that once was.

## What WTFight is NOT

Let's be clear about the lines we don't cross.

* **This is NOT a prediction market.** There is no referee, no final whistle, no "correct" answer. The fight doesn't end; conviction simply flows.
* **This is NOT a meme coin.** We aren't here for the ticker. Tokens are instruments of conviction, not jokes with a market cap.
* **This is NOT a casino.** There is no house. There is no edge. There is only a bonding curve that issues tokens and a scoreboard that tracks the battle.
