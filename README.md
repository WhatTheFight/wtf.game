## 🕹️ What is WTF.GAME?

**WTF.GAME** is an **experimental debate arena** where two camps fight across a full season.

Pick a side. Lock tokens in a **War Chest**. Each week, the scoreboard updates using a simple rule: **Market reality (TWAP) + Community conviction (Chest weight)**.

> Not a prediction market. Not "engagement farming."  
> It's a **public scoreboard for beliefs**.

## 💡 Core Mechanics

### **TWAP (50%)**
Time-weighted average price over 7 days. Sampled hourly (168 data points). Winner takes the TWAP point each week.

### **CHEST WEIGHT (50%)**
Tokens locked in each camp's War Chest. Heavier chest wins the conviction point. Snapshot-based (transparent & verifiable on-chain).

### **DOUBLE KO**
If TWAP and CHEST points split 1–1, both camps lose 10% from their chests. 96% goes to charity, 4% to ops.

### **Weekly Redistribution**
- **48%** to winning camp holders (pro-rata)
- **48%** to community-voted charity
- **4%** to operations & scaling

## 🎮 How to Play

### **Phase 1: Choose Your Camp**
1. Visit [whatthefight.github.io/wtf.game](https://whatthefight.github.io/wtf.game/)
2. Pick Camp A or Camp B
3. Buy the corresponding token on Pump.fun
4. Lock tokens in your camp's War Chest (public multisig)

### **Phase 2: Debate & Conviction**
1. Join the community (Discord coming soon)
2. Participate in weekly debates
3. Your locked tokens = voting weight
4. Every Sunday: new judgment applied

### **Phase 3: Settlement**
1. Weekly payouts computed on-chain
2. All transactions are public & auditable
3. Proceeds distributed pro-rata to winners
4. Losing camp contributes to charity
